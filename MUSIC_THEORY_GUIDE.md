# ğŸ“ Intervals

**Definition:** Distance between two notes

## Interval Numbers

Count letter names (inclusive):
- C to E = **3rd** (C-D-E)
- C to G = **5th** (C-D-E-F-G)
- C to C (octave) = **8th**

---

## Interval Quality

### Perfect Intervals
- Unison (P1): Same note
- Fourth (P4): 5 half steps
- Fifth (P5): 7 half steps
- Octave (P8): 12 half steps

### Major Intervals
- Second (M2): 2 half steps
- Third (M3): 4 half steps
- Sixth (M6): 9 half steps
- Seventh (M7): 11 half steps

### Minor Intervals
- Minor = Major minus 1 half step
- m2: 1 half step
- m3: 3 half steps
- m6: 8 half steps
- m7: 10 half steps

---

## Consonance vs Dissonance

**Consonant (Pleasant):**
- Perfect intervals (P1, P4, P5, P8)
- Major and minor 3rds and 6ths

**Dissonant (Tense):**
- Major and minor 2nds and 7ths
- Augmented and diminished intervals
